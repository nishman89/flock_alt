What's a Flock? 🐦

A Flock is what we call every event on the app. Just like birds of a feather flock together, a Flock is a group of like-minded people gathering around something they love - whether that's watching the football, hiking in the hills, or finding the best street food in the city.

Every Flock has a fixed number of spots, a time, a place, and a shared vibe. You join a Flock, show up, and meet your people. Simple as that.

How it works

1
Tell us what you're into

Football, gym, Indian food, gaming, hiking, live music - whatever makes you you. The more you share, the better your matches.

2
Set your preferences

Choose your city, how far you're willing to travel, and who you'd like to meet - girls, guys, or everyone.

3
Get matched to Flocks

Flock finds Flocks in your city that match your interests. These are real, organised Flocks planned in major cities across the UK.

4
Show up and meet your Flock

Join a Flock, meet people who are into the same things, and make genuine local friendships that last.
